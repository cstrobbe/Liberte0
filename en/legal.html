IT and freedom

Personal data collected

In France, personal data are protected by the law n° 78-87 of 6th of January 1978, the law n° 2004-801 of the 6th of August, the article L. 226-13 of the penal code and the European directive of the 24th of October 1995.

Liberté 0 collects user's personal information (name, email, contact details) only for the need of the services proposed on its websites, especially for the registration to discussion spaces using online forms or the statistic processing. The user gives these information in any case, especially when he proceeds himself to the input of such data. The mandatory information required by the user will be displayed.

Rectification of collected nominative information

In accordance to the dispositions of the article 34 of the law n° 48-47 of the 6th of January 1978, the user has the right to modify nominative data about himself. In order to do this, the user sends to Liberté 0 an email to the association (contact details provided above) indicating his name or coporate name, his contact details as well as the reference that he should have as a user on the Liberté 0 website. The modification will take place in sensible period one the application has been received.
